Privacy Policy – AJMI1 Calculator

Effective Date: 2025

The AJMI1 Calculator is a clinical decision-support application designed to assist healthcare professionals in evaluating cervical myelopathy severity using a structured scoring system. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles data and protects user privacy.

1. No Data Collection

AJMI1 does not collect, store, process, transmit, or share any personal, medical, or device data. All calculations take place entirely on the user’s device.

2. No Internet Communication

The app operates fully offline. It does not communicate with any external servers, APIs, analytics services, or third-party systems.

3. No Use of Third-Party SDKs

AJMI1 does not use advertising networks, analytics SDKs, tracking technologies, or third-party service libraries that collect data.

4. Data Storage

AJMI1 does not store or retain any information entered by the user. All input remains in temporary memory and is cleared when the app is closed or reset.

5. No Healthcare Information Handling

Because AJMI1 does not store or transmit any information, it does not handle, process, or manage protected health information (PHI) under HIPAA or other regulatory frameworks.

6. Children's Privacy

AJMI1 is intended for use by healthcare professionals and is not directed at children. The app does not collect any data from any users, including minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

If the functionality of the app changes in a way that affects data handling or privacy, this policy will be updated and made available through this same webpage.
